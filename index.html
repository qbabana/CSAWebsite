Our Mission

The Chinese Student Association is dedicated to creating an inclusive community where students of all backgrounds can celebrate, learn, and grow together. We honor Chinese cultural traditions while fostering meaningful connections across our diverse campus through cultural events, educational programs, and community service.

Our Vision

We envision a vibrant campus community where cultural exchange thrives, where students feel supported in their academic and personal journeys, and where the richness of Chinese heritage is appreciated and respected by all. Through intentional programming and meaningful dialogue, we aim to strengthen bonds within our community and across the entire university.

Organizational History

2015

Founded

Chinese Student Association established with a small group of dedicated students seeking to build community and celebrate heritage on campus.

2017

First Annual Spring Gala

Launch of our signature event, the Spring Gala, attracting over 200 attendees and becoming a hallmark of our programming.

2019

Committee Expansion

Introduced formalized committee structure to better serve our growing membership across cultural, academic, and community initiatives.

2022

Campus Recognition

Received formal recognition as a key cultural organization on campus, expanding our reach to over 5,000 engaged students annually.

2024

Strategic Partnerships

Established partnerships with academic departments and community organizations to integrate cultural education into broader campus initiatives.

── SIGNATURE EVENTS ──

Annual Spring Gala

Our flagship event celebrating Chinese culture with traditional music and dance performances, authentic cuisine, cultural displays, and a formal evening that honors our heritage and builds community.

Held annually: April • ~500 attendees • Formal attire

Lunar New Year Celebration

A vibrant festival featuring traditional performances, lion dances, cultural activities for all ages, festive decorations, and special cuisine. This colorful event welcomes the new year in style and brings our entire campus community together.

Held annually: February • Open to campus

Mid-Autumn Festival

Celebrate the harvest moon with traditional moon cakes, lantern displays, cultural performances, and outdoor festivities. This cherished event honors tradition while creating memorable moments for students and families.

Held annually: September/October • Family-friendly

Sponsorship & Partnership

合作与支持

The Chinese Student Association welcomes partnerships with organizations who share our commitment to cultural education, student development, and community engagement. Through structured programming and responsible stewardship, we offer sponsors meaningful visibility within a thoughtful academic setting.

Why Partner With CSA

Cultural Impact

CSA hosts curated programs reaching 9,000+ students annually across cultural, academic, and community initiatives.

Academic Alignment

Our events are designed in coordination with campus departments and uphold university standards of professionalism and inclusivity.
